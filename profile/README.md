# Welcome to Frutura! 🍎🥦

We're glad you're here! Frutura is dedicated to the **sustainable supply of fruits and vegetables**, and we believe that high-quality software development is a key driver in achieving that mission. Our engineering teams build reliable, scalable systems that help us deliver fresh produce efficiently and responsibly.

## Who We Are

At Frutura, we combine a passion for sustainability with a commitment to technical excellence. Through innovative software solutions, we continuously improve our processes — from supply chain management to logistics — ensuring that quality fruits and vegetables reach people in the most sustainable way possible.
